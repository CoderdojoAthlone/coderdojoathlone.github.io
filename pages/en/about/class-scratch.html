{{info.content | markdownify}} Scratch is a block-based visual programming language and online community targeted primarily at children. Users of the site can create online projects using a block-like interface.

Belt system

Kids are encouraged to realize their own projects and present them for their belt grading. Each level offers more challenges and opportunities to learn.
